Working rule

  • Cass keeps Peninsular sale proceeds after sale costs, mortgage and agreed Peninsular debts are paid.
  • The Seabrook value is locked at the agreed value for the N5-style option set.
  • From that locked Seabrook position onward, costs and benefits are shared 50 / 50 where that option applies.
  • At final Seabrook sale, Troy pays Cass $150,000 where that option applies.
  • No 70/30 or alternate split is active in the preferred current model.
  • Charlotte, school fees, super and unrelated adjustments are excluded.
  • Any lump sum from the house sale must stay in an offset account and is not for wasting or discretionary spending, except living expenses unless both agree.
Commercial modelling only. Formal legal drafting still needs to follow.
