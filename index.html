Working rule

  • Cass keeps Peninsular sale proceeds after sale costs and debt.
  • Troy keeps the fixed agreed Seabrook baseline valuation position.
  • Any Seabrook growth above that baseline is split 50 / 50.
  • Debt and sale costs are assumed to be paid first in settlement logic.
  • Troy-only business or discretionary spend reduces Troy’s side only.
  • Looking for work is explicitly excluded from that spend deduction rule.
Commercial modelling only. Formal legal drafting should still follow.
