Decision rule

Working commercial rule
  • Cass keeps Peninsular sale proceeds after sale costs and debt.
  • Troy keeps the fixed agreed Seabrook baseline valuation amount.
  • Any Seabrook growth above that baseline is split equally 50/50.
  • Debt and sale costs get cleared first in the final settlement model.
  • Troy-only business or discretionary spend is deducted from Troy’s side only.
  • Looking for work is not counted as discretionary spend.
This is still a modelling tool, not legal or financial advice. It is set up to reflect the rule you described, not to replace formal settlement drafting.
