## üì± How to Use (5 mins)

### Step 1: Open the Link
Tap the link above. The calculator loads instantly - no login needed.

### Step 2: Look at Overview
The first screen shows the key numbers:
- **Seabrook Value**: Current estimate
- **Peninsular Value**: Current estimate  
- **Combined Equity**: What we own outright
- **Recommended Option**: B2 (sell Pen, keep buffer)

### Step 3: Explore Options
Tap **"Options"** to see all 12 scenarios. Each card shows:
- What happens to debt
- What cash you'd have
- Risk level (green = safer)

**Tap any option** to mark it as your preference.

### Step 4: Compare Side-by-Side
Tap **"Compare"** to see all options in a table.

### Step 5: Check Growth Projections
Tap **"Growth"** to see what each property could be worth in 1, 3, 5, 10 years.

### Step 6: Ask Questions
Tap **"Q&A"** and type a question, or tap one of the quick buttons like "Safest option?"
